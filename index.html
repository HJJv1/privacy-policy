Last updated: 2025/12/10

Thank you for using apps published by H.C.C STUDIO, including but not limited to Life Note Planner.

To comply with global privacy regulations such as Taiwan PDPA, GDPR, CCPA, COPPA, and Google Play policies, we provide the following Privacy Policy.

Depending on the app features you use, we may collect:

  • GDPR — Applies to users in the European Union.

Our apps use the following services:
