Life Note Planner – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025/01/01

Thank you for using “Life Note Planner”. This service is available worldwide, including Taiwan, the United States, Japan, Korea, and the European Union. To comply with global privacy regulations such as Taiwan PDPA, GDPR, CCPA, COPPA, and Google Play policies, we provide the following Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information (Email via Firebase Authentication)
  • Usage data (Firebase Analytics)
  • Cloud data stored in Firestore (tasks, notes, anniversaries, finance, mood logs, and other content)
  • Images uploaded by users (stored in Firebase Storage)
  • Advertisement-related device information (Google AdMob)

2. Compliance with Global Regulations

  • Taiwan PDPA — Applies to users located in Taiwan.
  • GDPR — Applies to users in the European Union.
  • CCPA — Applies to users in California, USA.
  • COPPA — This app is not intended for children under 13.

3. Third-Party Services Used

This application uses the following services:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Storage
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Google AdMob

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide login and account management
  • Synchronize your cloud data across devices
  • Improve performance, stability, and user experience
  • Display personalized or non-personalized advertisements (AdMob)

5. Data Protection

All data is encrypted during transmission and securely stored using Firebase services. Only the authenticated user can access their personal data.

6. Children’s Privacy

This app is not designed for children under the age of 13. If a child attempts to use the app, parental consent is required.

7. Your Rights

  • Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Request access to your stored information
  • Export your data upon request
  • Stop using the application at any time
